MAXIFY APP
TEAM WORKLOAD & ASSIGNMENT SHEET
================================

PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
App that integrates with Spotify to dynamically change music based on:
- Geographical location
- Velocity (sports, running, cycling, etc.)
- Maybe other dependent on time

Team Size: 6–7 people
Stack:
- Frontend: React Native
- Backend: Node.js + Spotify Web API
